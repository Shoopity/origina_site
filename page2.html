The cabins are rustic and original.  Each cabin has a wood stove for heat and cooking.  There is no indoor plumbing.  Outhouses are conveniently located to each cabin.  There is a shower house.  Telephone service with local free and operator assisted long distance via calling cards or collect calls is available.  Well water and firewood are provided.
